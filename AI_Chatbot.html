AI Chatbot Integration

The Bot will text you on LINE and TG.
Just check your chat, and you’ll know what security incidents are happening in your company.

Intelligent Communication

Seamlessly integrated with LINE and Telegram, our AI Bot bridges the gap between complex security logs and actionable insights.

Virtual Analyst & Reporting

The AI bot sends a complete summary directly to your LINE or Telegram. It generates a simple report for the user, summarizing all the logs, and provides suggestions for improvement or identifies potential weaknesses in the system.

If users still find it confusing, they can also chat directly with the AI bot to ask for more details.

Real-time Notifications

Stay updated with instant notifications. The system tracks user actions and system events, providing a clear timeline of what happened, when it happened, and who was involved.

Rapid Incident Response

In urgent situations, such as detecting a ransomware attack, the Bot will immediately send an alert to the user’s Line and Telegram.

This allows the user to decide right away whether to stop the suspicious program, delete the file, or add it to a blacklist directly from the chat interface.
