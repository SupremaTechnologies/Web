Cloud Backup and Recovery System

Secure, reliable, and automated cloud backup solutions designed to protect your critical data from loss, ransomware, and disasters. Ensure business continuity with our advanced recovery systems.

Key Features

Our system provides comprehensive data protection with the following core capabilities:

Anomaly Detection

Real-time monitoring to detect mass deletion or encryption attempts instantly.

Ransomware Protection

Block contamination and secure your cloud storage from malicious uploads.

Instant Recovery

Auto-restore clean files immediately without IT intervention.

The Challenge & The Solution

Traditional backups leave you panicking when attacks happen. Our system changes the game.

The Threat: Ransomware Encryption

When ransomware strikes, your local files are encrypted, and these corrupted files are often synced to the cloud, compromising your backups. But we reject the upload, preserving your clean cloud backup.

The Solution: Instant Recovery

Our system detects the anomaly, blocks the upload of encrypted files, and automatically fetches clean versions from the cloud history. The files just reappear.
